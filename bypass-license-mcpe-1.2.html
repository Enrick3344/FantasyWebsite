    Bypass Minecraft 1.2 License

    Last updated 9/27/2017

    If you are an Android user and you are using an APK to play minecraft, you
    probably figured out that in the new Better Together 1.2 update,
    you aren't allowed to play if you don't have a license, which is only obtain
    by purchasing the app on Google Play Store. But today in this article, I will show
    you a way to bypass that license a play minecraft in the new version!

    First of all you will need to allow Unknown Sources on your settings
    if it's not already done.
    Go in Settings>Security> and enable Unknown Sources.

    What you will need

    - To be able to bypass the license you will need to download the Lucky Patcher app.
    hit the lucky patcher to automaticly download the apk.

    - Then you will have to of course, download the Minecraft APK! Click Minecraft App
    automaticly download the apk!
    * * * NOTE * * * Do Not Open Minecraft when it's done download, else it won't work!

    Instructions

    1 . When you installed the Lucky Patcher app and the Minecraft app, you are ready
    to begin! First of all open the Lucky Patcher app.
    There will be a loading screen and then a list of app will appear!

    2 . Once you see the app list, you will have to find the minecraft one and hit it!
    A little menu will open under the app, you will then
    hit Open Menu of Patches.

    3 . Then you will hit the Create Modified APK File window. If the app tell you
    that theres a new update of Lucky Patcher , hit no and then
    hit the Create Modified APK File menu.

    4 . After doing step 3, a menu will open with 5 option! Select the first one
    called APK without License Verification Following that
    will be another menu!

    5 . In that menu there will be a lot of choices! Do Not Touch Anything!
    And hit Rebuild The App then you wait and when the loading
    is done you can leave the LuckyPatcher app and go to your minecraft! without any License message!

    Enjoy everyone and hope to see you all in Fantasy Island soon!
