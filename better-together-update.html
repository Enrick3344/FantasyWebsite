    Better Together Update

    Last updated 9/25/2017

    As you all probably know, Minecraft launched a new update called
    the better together update. this update brought a lot of changes to the multiplayer
    world and a bunch of new features in single player.
    As you guys probably noticed, you now have to create or login
    into a Xbox Live account to be able to play online.
    Some of you guys won't be able to get their old account name into their Xbox account.
    If you don't have your old name, don't worry! The owner created a plugin
    that allows you guys to get your good ol' account back.
    When you will join the game you have to hit pause and then hit settings
    After you will see "Name Changing Menu". enter that menu and enter your old account name.
    Once that's done, put the switch to on and quit the menu. It will ask you a password
    which is the password of your old account. Enter that password and voila!
    each time you will join the server you will automaticly join with that old name!
    Have fun and enjoy the Update!
